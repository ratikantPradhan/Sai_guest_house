COMMON QUESTIONS

FAQ

What are your check-in and check-out times?+
Standard check-in from 12:00 PM and check-out by 11:00 AM. Early/late check-in may be arranged on request.
Is parking available?+
Yes, safe and free parking is available for all guests on the premises.
Do you serve meals?+
Meals can be arranged on request. Please inform us at the time of booking.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept Cash, UPI, Card Swipe, and Net Banking.
Are group or event bookings available?+
Yes, we have a Conference Hall (capacity 50) for group stays and events. Call us to discuss requirements.
How do I get here from Bhubaneswar?+
We are on NH-57 in Daspalla, approximately 100 km from Bhubaneswar. Reachable by bus, taxi, or private vehicle.
